Types of Forklift Crimes

ATM Heist

ATM robberies are quite possibly the most common crime commited with the use of forklifts. The concept of them is simple. One person steals a forklift from a nearby construction site and rams it into an ATM machine, putting it inside a chevy truck driven by a second person and driving away.
